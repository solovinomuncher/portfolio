About Me

My name is Erick. I build and design websites. I live in San Diego, California.

I like building things; both tangible things like woodworking and 3D printing projects, and intangible things like stories and websites.

However, building tangible things cost money, whereas websites are absolutely free and require only a computer. While I can only build so many tangible things, with intangibles I can keep building and building and building...

Hence why I'm a web developer and not a woodworker.

About My Projects

Recipe Cost Management App

My first web app was a recipe cost management app for my mom. Spreadsheets weren't cutting it, so I decided to flex my web development skills and make an app. She makes pastries and wanted to calculate the cost of ingredients per recipe and adjust quantites if needed. It took a good amount of domain knowledge (imperial vs metric units, conversions, volume vs weight, etc), but I got it to work!
